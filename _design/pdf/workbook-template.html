Answer Key

Solutions

Exercise 1

$2x + 5 = 17$

$2x = 12$

Answer: $x = 6$

Exercise 2

$f(4) = 3(4)^2 - 2(4) + 1$

$f(4) = 3(16) - 8 + 1$

$f(4) = 48 - 8 + 1$

Answer: $f(4) = 41$

Exercise 3

$\frac{x^2 - 9}{x + 3} = \frac{(x+3)(x-3)}{x + 3}$

Answer: $x - 3$ (where $x \neq -3$)

Key Takeaways

  • Always check your answers by substituting back into the original equation
  • Watch for restrictions in the domain (like division by zero)
  • Factor completely before simplifying fractions
