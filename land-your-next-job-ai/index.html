Course Overview

What you'll get from this bootcamp

Who it's for

  • ✓ Job seekers who've been searching for 3+ months without traction
  • ✓ Non-technical professionals in ops, marketing, sales, and communications
  • ✓ Anyone applying broadly instead of targeting strategically
  • ✓ People who know AI exists but aren't using it beyond basics (73% of job seekers)

What you'll walk away with

  • ✓ A complete AI-powered job search system you can run independently
  • ✓ 6 tangible deliverables: not just knowledge, but actual job search assets
  • ✓ Hands-on AI walkthroughs designed for non-technical users
  • ✓ Peer feedback and accountability from your cohort
  • ✓ Templates, prompts, and scripts you'll reuse for years

This bootcamp is built for people whose #1 problem is strategy, not materials. You probably already have a resume. What you need is a system for finding the right roles, getting noticed by the right people, and converting conversations into offers. AI is the multiplier that makes this possible at a scale that wasn't available even a year ago.

01

Career Targeting

Strategy & AI Job Matching

Define your ideal role, use AI to batch-analyze job postings, and build a personal matching agent that surfaces the right opportunities daily.

✓

Deliverable: AI matching prompt + curated list of 10 target roles

A copy-paste-ready AI prompt calibrated to your role-fit criteria, plus a shortlist of 10 high-match roles with company, title, match rationale, and link.

  • 15 min

    Reflect on your career criteria

    Write down what matters most across four dimensions: function, culture, growth, and compensation. Bring this to Session 1.

  • 20 min

    Gather 5-10 job postings you find interesting

    Browse LinkedIn, Indeed, or your preferred job board. Save 5-10 postings that caught your eye. We'll use these as raw material for AI analysis.

  • 5 min

    Set up a free AI tool

    Create an account on ChatGPT or Claude (free tier). We'll use this throughout the bootcamp.

Total: ~40 min

  • 5 min

    Welcome & Introductions

    Meet your cohort, bootcamp overview, what you'll walk away with today.

  • 10 min

    The Strategy Problem

    Why spray-and-pray fails. 59% of job seekers search 3+ months. The shift from "fix my resume" to "fix my strategy."

  • 15 min

    Role-Fit Framework

    Four dimensions: function fit, culture fit, growth trajectory, compensation. Fill in your own criteria worksheet.

  • 20 min

    Live AI Demo: Job Matching

    Instructor feeds 50+ job postings into AI with role-fit criteria. Walk through prompt engineering. Surface top 5 matches.

  • 25 min

    Hands-On Workshop

    Build your own AI job matching prompt. Test with real job boards. Refine criteria in real time. Peer feedback in breakout rooms.

  • 10 min

    Building Your Daily Agent

    Set up your prompt to run daily: saved prompts, custom GPTs, automation options.

  • 5 min

    Wrap-Up & Preview

    Review deliverable checklist. Preview Session 2. Between-session assignments.

  • 10 min/day

    Run your AI matching agent daily for one week

    Paste your prompt into ChatGPT or Claude each day with fresh job postings. Note which roles score highest and why.

  • 15 min

    Refine your role-fit criteria

    After a few days, adjust your criteria. Too many matches? Tighten your requirements. Too few? Broaden one dimension.

  • 20 min

    Finalize your top 10 target roles

    Lock in your shortlist of 10 roles and identify your top 3 dream companies. Bring your current resume to Session 2.

Total: ~1.5 hrs over the week

→ Bring your finalized top 10 roles and current resume to Session 2, where we'll optimize your resume for your #1 target role.

Resume & ATS

Get Past the Algorithms

Demystify how ATS systems actually filter applications, use AI to gap-analyze your resume against target postings, and build an optimized resume that gets through.

✓

Deliverable: 1 ATS-optimized resume tailored to your target role

A fully revised resume that passes ATS keyword matching for your #1 target role, with a gap analysis showing exactly what changed and why.

  • 5 min

    Read: How ATS actually works

    Read the provided 5-minute primer on Applicant Tracking Systems. Most of what you've heard is myth.

  • 10 min

    Pick your #1 target role from your shortlist

    Choose the single role you'd most like to apply to. Copy the full job posting text into a document.

  • 5 min

    Prepare your current resume

    Have your resume ready as a Google Doc or Word file (not PDF). We'll be making real edits during the session.

Total: ~20 min

  • 5 min

    Check-In

    How did your AI agent perform this week? Quick wins and surprises from daily matching.

  • 10 min

    ATS Demystified

    How ATS systems actually parse resumes. What gets filtered and why. The 80/20 of what recruiters scan.

  • 15 min

    Live AI Demo: Gap Analysis

    Feed a job posting + sample resume into AI. Get a detailed gap analysis: missing keywords, weak bullet points, formatting issues.

  • 35 min

    Hands-On: Optimize Your Resume

    Run your own gap analysis. Rewrite bullet points using AI. Peer review in breakout rooms.

  • 15 min

    The Multi-Resume Strategy

    How to create role-specific resume variants efficiently. AI templating for quick customization.

  • 10 min

    Wrap-Up

    Deliverable check. Preview Session 3. Homework assignments.

  • 30 min

    Finalize your ATS-optimized resume

    Incorporate all feedback. Run one final AI gap analysis to verify keyword coverage.

  • 20 min

    Create one resume variant

    Pick a second role from your top 10 and use the AI templating approach to create a variant.

  • 10 min

    Audit your LinkedIn profile

    Screenshot your current headline, summary, and experience section. Note what feels outdated or generic.

Total: ~1 hr

→ Bring your LinkedIn audit screenshots to Session 3, where we'll rebuild your profile and craft your personal brand.
03

LinkedIn & Personal Brand

Get Visible to the Right People

Optimize your LinkedIn profile from headline to skills section, develop a content strategy that positions you as AI-savvy, and publish your first brand-building post.

✓

Deliverable: Refreshed LinkedIn profile + 1 published post

An AI-optimized LinkedIn headline, summary, and skills section, plus one published LinkedIn post that showcases your expertise and AI fluency.

  • 15 min

    Find 3 LinkedIn profiles you admire

    Look at people in roles similar to your targets. Note specific headlines, summary structures, or featured sections that stand out.

  • 10 min

    Draft your "I help X do Y" statement

    In one sentence, describe what you do and who benefits. Example: "I help SaaS companies turn customer data into retention strategies."

  • 5 min

    Watch: The LinkedIn algorithm in 5 minutes

    Watch the provided short video on how LinkedIn's feed algorithm decides what to show.

Total: ~30 min

  • 5 min

    Check-In

    Resume wins from the week. Anyone submit an application with their new resume?

  • 10 min

    Profile Audit: What Recruiters See

    Walk through a recruiter's 6-second scan. Common profile mistakes that cost you visibility.

  • 15 min

    Live AI Demo: Profile Rewrite

    Take a volunteer's real LinkedIn headline and summary. Use AI to generate 5 variants. Discuss which works.

  • 25 min

    Hands-On: Rebuild Your Profile

    Rewrite your headline, summary, and top 3 experience bullets using AI. Update skills section. Peer review in pairs.

  • 20 min

    Content Strategy: Post Like a Pro

    Why posting matters for job seekers. The 3 post formats: insight, story, question. Draft your first post.

  • 15 min

    Publish & Wrap-Up

    Final edits on your post. Hit publish live (accountability!). Preview Session 4.

  • 30 min

    Finish updating your full LinkedIn profile

    Complete all sections: headline, summary, experience, skills, and featured section.

  • 20 min

    Write and publish a second LinkedIn post

    Use one of the 3 formats. Aim for a post that demonstrates industry knowledge.

  • 20 min

    Identify 15 people to connect with

    Find 15 people on LinkedIn: 5 peers, 5 hiring managers, 5 connectors. Bring this list to Session 4.

Total: ~1 hr 10 min

→ Bring your list of 15 target connections to Session 4, where we'll craft personalized outreach messages and practice informational interviews.
04

Networking & Outreach

Build Connections That Open Doors

Master the art of warm outreach, use AI to personalize connection messages at scale without sounding robotic, and practice informational interviews that lead to referrals.

✓

Deliverable: 10 personalized outreach messages + informational interview script

Ten ready-to-send LinkedIn messages tailored to specific people at your target companies, plus a tested informational interview script with follow-up templates.

  • 25 min

    Research 5 people from your connection list

    For 5 of your 15 targets, read their recent posts, note their career path, find something specific you can reference.

  • 10 min

    Write one outreach message on your own (no AI)

    Write a genuine connection request message. We'll compare your natural voice to AI-generated versions in session.

  • 5 min

    Read: The anatomy of a great cold message

    Read the provided 3-minute guide. Focus on the framework: specific hook, clear ask, easy out.

Total: ~40 min

  • 5 min

    Check-In

    LinkedIn profile updates and post engagement. Any unexpected connections or responses?

  • 10 min

    The Networking Mindset Shift

    Why networking isn't "using people." How 70% of jobs are filled through connections.

  • 15 min

    Live AI Demo: Personalized Outreach

    Take 3 real profiles. Compare AI-only vs. AI-assisted (your research + AI writing). Show the quality difference.

  • 25 min

    Hands-On: Craft Your Messages

    Write 10 personalized outreach messages. Each must reference something specific about the recipient.

  • 20 min

    Informational Interview Masterclass

    The 5-question framework that gets referrals. Role-play practice in pairs. AI follow-up templates.

  • 15 min

    Wrap-Up

    Deliverable check: 10 messages ready to send. Preview Session 5.

  • 15 min

    Send all 10 outreach messages

    Don't let them sit in drafts. Send within 48 hours. Track who responds.

  • 1 hr

    Conduct 1-2 informational interviews

    If connections respond, schedule and complete at least one using the 5-question framework. Follow up within 24 hours.

  • 30 min

    Build research dossiers for your top 3 companies

    Use AI to compile: recent news, financial health, culture signals, team structure, open roles.

Total: ~1 hr 45 min

→ Bring your company research dossiers and any informational interview insights to Session 5, where we'll turn research into interview preparation.

Company Research & Interviews

Show Up Prepared, Stand Out

Build an AI-powered company research system, turn intelligence into interview talking points, and practice behavioral interviews using the STAR method with AI coaching.

✓

Deliverable: Research dossier for top 3 companies + 5 prepared interview answers

Detailed company dossiers covering culture, strategy, challenges, and team structure, plus 5 polished STAR-method answers to common behavioral questions.

  • 15 min

    Review your company research dossiers

    Fill any gaps: do you know each company's biggest challenge? Their most recent product launch? Their hiring manager's background?

  • 20 min

    List 5 work accomplishments you're proud of

    Times you solved a hard problem, led a project, improved a process, or handled a difficult situation. 1-2 sentences each.

  • 10 min

    Watch: The STAR method in action

    Watch the provided 8-minute video showing strong vs. weak STAR responses.

Total: ~45 min

  • 5 min

    Check-In

    Networking wins: any responses, informational interviews, or referrals to share?

  • 10 min

    Research-to-Interview Pipeline

    How to turn company research into "I understand your business" talking points.

  • 15 min

    Live AI Demo: Company Dossier Deep-Dive

    Enrich a dossier with AI: leadership analysis, competitive landscape, recent press. Generate company-specific interview questions.

  • 30 min

    Hands-On: STAR Method Workshop

    Transform your 5 accomplishments into polished STAR answers using AI. Mock interview rounds in pairs.

  • 15 min

    The Curveball Questions

    Handling "tell me about yourself," salary questions, gaps, and "why are you leaving?" When NOT to use AI.

  • 15 min

    Wrap-Up

    Deliverable check: dossiers enriched, 5 STAR answers polished. Preview Session 6.

  • 45 min

    Complete 2 full mock interviews with AI

    Do one behavioral interview and one role-specific interview for your #1 target. Record yourself if possible.

  • 30 min

    Polish your STAR answer bank

    Expand from 5 to 8-10 prepared answers covering: leadership, conflict, failure, teamwork, initiative, problem-solving.

  • 20 min

    Research salary ranges for your target roles

    Use Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, Payscale, or AI. Note the low, median, and high for each.

Total: ~1 hr 35 min

Negotiation & Launch

Close Strong, Stay Systematic

Master salary negotiation with AI-powered research and scripts, build strategic follow-up sequences, and assemble everything into a sustainable weekly job search system.

    Compile your salary research

    Organize salary data: role, company, range (low/mid/high), source. Identify your target number and walk-away number.

  • 10 min

    Reflect on your bootcamp journey

    Review what you've built across Sessions 1-5. Note any gaps or items you want to strengthen.

  • 5 min

    Write your biggest job search fear

    What still makes you nervous? Salary talk? Rejection? Imposter syndrome? We'll address top fears in session.

    Salary Negotiation Foundations

    Build a complete script: initial response, counter-offer email, verbal talking points. Multiple scenarios.

  • 15 min

    Hands-On: Build Your Scripts

    The Follow-Up System
